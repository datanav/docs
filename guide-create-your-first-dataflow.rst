Create Your First Dataflow
==========================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1
   :hidden:

   Collect <tutorial-getting-started-collect>
   Enrich
   Connect
   Transform
   Share

In order for you to get a feeling for how you will be working with Sesam, in projects, we have created the above listed tutorials. These will help you create your first Sesam Dataflow to get you started. 

This step-by-step guide will help you create your first dataflow.

.. admonition::  Objectives:
   
   After you complete these tutorials you will have:

   #. Collected to two source systems and setup inbound pipes for each
   #. Defined namespaces and added rdf-type in the enrich step
   #. Connected your system data in a global pipe and have created global properties
   #. Transformed data based on rdf-type and binding global properties to target schema
   #. Share your data and validate that company data has been sent to HubSpot
